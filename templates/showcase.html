Design system inspiration of Sequora

A near-black canvas anchored on Graphite Black #0B0F14 with Sora Extrabold display headlines in confident negative tracking. Brand voltage is Tech Cyan #1FD3E0 applied at 5–10% density on CTAs, focus rings, active toggles, and the timeline playhead.

00 — LOGO LOCKUP

Brand mark · symbol + wordmark

Symbol-to-wordmark proportion locked at X : 3.2X. Clear-space equals X on every side. Minimum sizes: 20mm print · 120px digital. Dark is the canonical canvas; light, mono-white, mono-black are inverse applications.

09 — DIRECTIVES

Do's and don'ts

The Don'ts encode the negative space of the brand — they tell an agent what NOT to default to.

✓ DO

  • Canvas is Graphite Black #0B0F14 — near-black with a graphite tint, never pure black.
  • Reserve Tech Cyan for primary CTAs, focus rings, active toggles, brand mark, timeline playhead. Aim for 5–10% density.
  • Sora Extrabold (800) with negative tracking on hero headlines. Eyebrow uses uppercase + +2.5px tracking.
  • Build depth with surface lift and 1px hairlines — step from canvas → surface-1 → surface-2.
  • Mono only for technical readouts — timecode, paths, file sizes, frame counts.
  • Use the official cyan gradient (Deep Cyan → Tech Cyan) for glows, motion lines, waveforms — always linear.
  • Maintain logo clear-space of X on every side; minimum sizes 20mm print / 120px digital.

✕ DON'T

  • Don't use pure #000000 as canvas. The graphite-blue cast is intentional and prevents harshness.
  • Don't introduce a second chromatic accent — no purple, pink, orange, green-as-decoration.
  • Don't paint cyan on body text, large fills, or section backgrounds. Cyan is signal light, not wallpaper.
  • Don't pill-round primary CTAs. Buttons stay rounded.md 8px. Pill is for toggles and status.
  • Don't apply neutral gray drop shadows. Depth is surface + hairline + selective cyan glow.
  • Don't put white text on Tech Cyan. The only ink on cyan is on-primary #0B0F14.
  • Don't use Sora for body text. Below headline (24px), always Inter.
  • Don't ship light-mode marketing as default. Light is inverse application — print, monochrome.
